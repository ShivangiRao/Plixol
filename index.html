Review Management

Aggregate Your Product Reviews

This tool scrapes all negative reviews placed on a product of a seller. The seller will add the item ID to monitor and the application will pull all its negative reviews and saves to database. It then goes to seller central, scrapes the order pages and checks under which Order, the seller has received a negative review.
